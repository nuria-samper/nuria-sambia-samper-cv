### 🔍 Highlighted Use Cases:

- **[App Deferred Payment Innovation for Banc Sabadell](https://www.notion.so/App-Deferred-Payments-216ceebb8e1181c7a00adcb320672e51?pvs=21)**
    
    Spearheaded the cross-functional product value proposition, delivery, and roadmap for this innovative deferred payment solution for bills and payments within the bank. This resulted in **70%** of eligible payments being **successfully deferred** with a **default risk ratio of less than 0.8%**, surpassing the initially assigned minimums.
    
- [**Marketplace Loyalty Program**](https://www.notion.so/Marketplace-Loyalty-Program-216ceebb8e11810b92d4e999af7a6977?pvs=21)
    
    Collaborated on defining business rules and led the development, testing (including A/B testing), and iteration of a B2C loyalty program. Resulting in an increase in **purchase value** per user (PVPU) from 40€ to **98€** and a **user adoption rate** of **68%** across 11.5 million active users.
    
- [Chatbot for point redemption issues in existing orders](https://www.notion.so/Marketplace-chatboot-216ceebb8e118103827efec791226b88?pvs=21)
    
    Led the first innovation project to improve customer support after working hours and reduce operational costs. Initial results after 2 months showed a **25% reduction in Jira tickets** related to point redemption after an order and a **5% decrease in call center inquiries** from loyalty users.
    
- [Online Car Price Assessment Tool in multiple European markets](https://www.notion.so/Online-Car-Price-Assessment-Tool-216ceebb8e11819b88a6ed5c0e24576d?pvs=21)
    
    Led the release plan in 3 countries; collaborated with user research (UX/UI); MVP delivery, and iteration testing for a second-hand vehicle listing product. Scaling from **0 to 90 dealership appointments** and weekly **qualified leads ratio >15** in Germany.
    
- [Product operational systems changes](https://www.notion.so/Global-operational-systems-changes-216ceebb8e1181d68f81c6b83cf2e674?pvs=21)
    
    Led **4 globally distributed teams** in discovering and delivering solutions for sales, ops, staffing and finance in the company **before the initial public offering (IPO)**.
